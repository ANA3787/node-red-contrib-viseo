Billions of people are using messaging platforms everyday to perform simple task, getting helps, stay informed, everywhere, anytime.

Focus on User Experience

Because 75% of success relies on the conversational UX, we built a tool for Bot Owner (scenarist or ethnologist) to manipulate components of a conversational flow.

Modular and Open-source

Reliable, scalable and secure

The framework can be package into a Docker container and follow best pratices provides by Microsoft, Facebook and IBM. All critical data are crypted and message are secured through HTTPs connection.
